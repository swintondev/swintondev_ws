About you

We're looking for an excellent Next.js/React engineer, with experience designing and building large, consumer facing applications for a wide audience. The ideal person would:

  • Be able to build the front end for a large, consumer focused web application themselves
  • Be confident creating a product used by thousands of people
  • Be able to make that application performant, accessible and beautiful
  • Be a strong communicator, able to express and work through complex technical issues clearly with a team
  • Be able to contribute on the backend where necessary

We are a small team with a lot to do, so the ideal candidate won't mind getting stuck into the highest priority engineering problems to make the product a success.
