Job description

We are a funded, stealth-mode startup looking for an excellent front end focused developer to join our team as a founding member. You will have wide ranging input into our product's design & architecture.

We're building a brand new, consumer facing, web app with a modern stack. You will be working closely with our lead engineer to design, architect and deliver the site. Your work will be used by hundreds of thousands of people, and will be crucial to ensuring we convert leads into customers. As a new startup we will expect you to help choose the right technologies and approaches.

Our team has a track record of achieving radical change in our target industry. We have identified an unparalleled opportunity to transform the sector and improve the lives of millions of people.

About you

We're looking for an excellent Next.js/React engineer, with experience designing and building large, consumer facing applications for a wide audience. The ideal candidate would:

  • Have 3+ years of experience building mass market applications
  • Be a self-starter, who is happy to work independently
  • Have an eye for detail and an interest in design, even if you're not primarily a designer
  • Have experience building sites that load lightning fast, achieve high conversion rates, and support effective SEO
  • Have experience solving problems in a fast moving and growing company
  • Have experience working on mobile-first applications that target a wide range of platforms and browsers
  • Be a strong communicator, able to express and work through complex technical issues clearly with a team

You might also:

  • Have an understanding of backend engineering and the ability to contribute as a full-stack engineer if necessary
  • Have experience with user testing, and incorporating user feedback into designs

We are a small team with a lot to do, so the ideal candidate won't mind getting stuck into whatever engineering problems we need to make the product a success.
